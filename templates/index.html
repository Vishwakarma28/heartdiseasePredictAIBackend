Usage Example

Using cURL:

curl -X POST https://your-api-url/predict \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "Age": 42,
    "Sex": "M",
    "ChestPainType": "ASY",
    "RestingBP": 160.0,
    "Cholesterol": 193.0,
    "FastingBS": 0,
    "RestingECG": "Normal",
    "MaxHR": 102,
    "ExerciseAngina": "Y",
    "Oldpeak": 3.0,
    "ST_Slope": "Flat"
  }'

Parameters

  • Age: Age of the patient
  • Sex: M (Male) or F (Female)
  • ChestPainType: ASY, NAP, ATA, or TA
  • RestingBP: Resting blood pressure (mm Hg)
  • Cholesterol: Serum cholesterol (mg/dl)
  • FastingBS: Fasting blood sugar (1 if > 120 mg/dl, 0 otherwise)
  • RestingECG: Normal, ST, or LVH
  • MaxHR: Maximum heart rate achieved
  • ExerciseAngina: Y (Yes) or N (No)
  • Oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise
  • ST_Slope: Up, Flat, or Down
